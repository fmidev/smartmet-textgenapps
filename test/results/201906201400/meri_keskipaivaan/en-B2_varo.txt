Weather report for the Quark on Thursday 14 o'clock

Expected weather until Friday noon:

Mainly southerly wind 2-6 m/s.
Strengthening south to south-easterly wind before midnight, from early Friday morning 7-11 m/s.


Rain showers in some places and thunderstorms are also likely.
On Friday forenoon the weather is half cloudy and fair.


Good visibility.
