Weather report for Southern Bay of Bothnia on Sunday 7 o'clock

Expected weather until Monday morning:

Strengthening west to south-westerly wind, gradually in the beginning 6-8 m/s, from the afternoon westerly wind 9-13 m/s, in the evening at its strongest 15 m/s.


The weather is half cloudy and fair.
On Sunday afternoon rain showers in some places.
In the evening and at night the weather is half cloudy and fair.


Good visibility.
