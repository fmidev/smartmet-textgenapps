Weather report for Southern Bay of Bothnia on Sunday 7 o'clock

Expected weather until Monday morning:

Strengthening west to south-westerly wind, gradually in the beginning 6-9 m/s, from the afternoon westerly wind 10-14 m/s, at its strongest 16 m/s.


The weather is half cloudy and fair.
On Sunday afternoon rain or snow showers in some places.
In the evening and at night the weather is half cloudy and fair.


On Sunday afternoon rain or snow showers in some places, mostly good visibility.
