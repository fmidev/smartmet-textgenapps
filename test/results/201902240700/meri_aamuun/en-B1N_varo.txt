Weather report for Northern Bay of Bothnia on Sunday 7 o'clock

Expected weather until Monday morning:

Strengthening west to south-westerly wind, gradually in the beginning 6-9 m/s, from the afternoon westerly wind, on Monday small hours 11-15 m/s, at its strongest 17 m/s.


The weather is half cloudy and fair.
On Sunday afternoon rain showers in many places.
In the evening and at night the weather is half cloudy and fair.


Good visibility.
