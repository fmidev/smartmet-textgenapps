Weather report for Northern Bay of Bothnia on Sunday 7 o'clock

Expected weather until Monday morning:

Strengthening westerly wind, gradually in the beginning 6-8 m/s, from the late evening 10-14 m/s, at its strongest 17 m/s.
On Monday small hours north-westerly wind.


In the beginning the weather is half cloudy and fair.
On Sunday afternoon rain showers in many places.
In the evening and at night the weather is half cloudy and fair.


Good visibility.
