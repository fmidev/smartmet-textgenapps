Weather report for Bay of Bothnia on Sunday 7 o'clock

Expected weather until Monday morning:

Strengthening west to south-westerly wind, gradually in the beginning 6-9 m/s, from the afternoon westerly wind 10-14 m/s, at its strongest 17 m/s.
In the beginning the weather is half cloudy and fair.
On Sunday afternoon rain showers in some places.
In the evening and at night the weather is half cloudy and fair.
Good visibility.
