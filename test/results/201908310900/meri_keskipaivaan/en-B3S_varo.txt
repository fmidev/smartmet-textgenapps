Weather report for Southern Bothnian Sea on Saturday 9 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Weakening south to south-westerly wind, in the beginning 8-12 m/s, from the evening southerly wind, before midnight 6-8 m/s.
Strengthening wind from early Sunday morning, in the forenoon 8-11 m/s.


In the beginning the weather is clear.
On Sunday forenoon rain showers in some places, in some places thunder is also possible.


Good visibility.
