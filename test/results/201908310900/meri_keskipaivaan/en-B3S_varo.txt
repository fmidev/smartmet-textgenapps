Weather report for Southern Bothnian Sea on Saturday 9 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Weakening south to south-westerly wind, gradually in the beginning 8-12 m/s, before midnight southerly wind 6-8 m/s.
Gradually strengthening wind from early Sunday morning, in the forenoon 8-12 m/s.


The weather is rather clear and fair.
On Sunday forenoon rain showers in some places, in some places thunder is also possible.


Good visibility.
