Weather report for Sea of Bothnia on Saturday 9 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Weakening south to south-westerly wind, in the beginning 8-12 m/s, before midnight 6-8 m/s.
Strengthening southerly wind from early Sunday morning, in the forenoon 8-11 m/s.
In the beginning the weather is clear.
On Sunday forenoon possibly occasional rain showers, in some places thunder is also possible.
Good visibility.
