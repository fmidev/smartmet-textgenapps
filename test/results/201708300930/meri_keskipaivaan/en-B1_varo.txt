Weather report for Bay of Bothnia on Wednesday 9 o'clock

Expected weather until Thursday noon:

Strengthening west to south-westerly wind, in the beginning 2-5 m/s, before midnight 8-12 m/s, at its strongest 14 m/s.
Weakening wind from early Thursday morning, in the forenoon 7-11 m/s.


Rain showers in some places, some thunder also occurs.
The weather becomes fair and is clear from the afternoon.


Rain showers in some places, some thunder also occurs, mostly good visibility.
