Weather report for Southern Bay of Bothnia on Wednesday 9 o'clock

Expected weather until Thursday noon:

Strengthening west to south-westerly wind, in the beginning 2-5 m/s, in the evening 9-13 m/s.
Gradually weakening wind from the late evening, on Thursday morning 5-9 m/s.


Rain showers in some places, some thunder also occurs.
From the afternoon the weather is clear.


Good visibility.
