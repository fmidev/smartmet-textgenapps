Weather report for Sea of Archipelago on Monday 15 o'clock

Expected weather until Tuesday evening:

Strengthening east to north-easterly wind, in the beginning 10-15 m/s, from Tuesday forenoon 11-16 m/s, at its strongest 19 m/s.


The weather is rather clear and fair.
In the morning and during the day light snowfall in many places.
