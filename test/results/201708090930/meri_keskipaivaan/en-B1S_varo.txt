Weather report for Southern Bay of Bothnia on Wednesday 9 o'clock

Expected weather until Thursday noon:

South-westerly wind 4-8 m/s.
From the late evening south to south-easterly wind.


The weather is half cloudy and fair.
On Wednesday afternoon and evening possibly occasional rain showers.
The weather becomes fair and is comparatively cloudy in the evening and at night.
From midnight light rain in some places.


Good visibility.
