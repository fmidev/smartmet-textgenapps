Weather report for Western Gulf of Finland on Saturday 10 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Variable wind 2-6 m/s.
Gradually strengthening south to south-easterly wind from the evening, on Sunday small hours 8-12 m/s.
In the forenoon south-westerly wind.


The weather is half cloudy and fair.
In the forenoon possibly dense fog in some places.
At night and in the morning rain showers in many places, some thunder also occurs.
Showers can be heavy after midnight and in the morning.


At night and in the morning rain showers in some places, some thunder also occurs, otherwise good visibility.
