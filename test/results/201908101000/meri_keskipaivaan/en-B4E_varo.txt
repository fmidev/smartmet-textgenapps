Weather report for Sea of Archipelago on Saturday 10 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Strengthening southerly wind, gradually in the beginning 2-4 m/s, from early Sunday morning west to south-westerly wind 7-11 m/s, at its strongest 13 m/s.


From forenoon rain showers in many places, some thunder also occurs.
Showers can be heavy after midnight.
