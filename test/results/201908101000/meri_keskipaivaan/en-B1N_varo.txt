Weather report for Northern Bay of Bothnia on Saturday 10 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Variable wind 1-4 m/s.
Gradually strengthening wind from forenoon, in the evening east to south-easterly wind 3-7 m/s, on Sunday small hours 7-11 m/s.


The weather is rather clear and fair.
In the morning and during the day rain showers in many places.


Good visibility.
