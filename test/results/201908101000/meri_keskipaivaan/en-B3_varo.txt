Weather report for Sea of Bothnia on Saturday 10 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Strengthening south to south-easterly wind, gradually in the beginning 3-6 m/s, from the evening 5-9 m/s, at its strongest 11 m/s.


The weather is rather clear and fair.
From the afternoon rain showers in many places, in some places some thunder also occurs.
Showers can be heavy after midnight and in the morning.
