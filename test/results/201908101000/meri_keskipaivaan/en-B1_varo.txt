Weather report for Bay of Bothnia on Saturday 10 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Variable wind 2-6 m/s.
Gradually strengthening mainly easterly wind from the afternoon, before midnight east to south-easterly wind 5-9 m/s, at its strongest 11 m/s.


In the beginning the weather is clear.
On Saturday afternoon possibly occasional rain showers.
In the evening and at night the weather is rather clear.
From midnight rain showers in some places.


Good visibility.
