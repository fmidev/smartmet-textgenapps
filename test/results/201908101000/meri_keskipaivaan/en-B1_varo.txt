Weather report for Bay of Bothnia on Saturday 10 o'clock

Expected weather until Sunday noon:

Mainly easterly wind 1-4 m/s.
Strengthening wind from the afternoon, in the evening east to south-easterly wind 5-9 m/s, at its strongest 11 m/s.


The weather is rather clear and fair.
From midnight rain showers in many places.


Good visibility.
