Weather report for Sea of Bothnia on Monday 13 o'clock

Expected weather until Tuesday noon:

Mainly westerly wind 5-10 m/s.
Mainly south-westerly wind from the late evening.
Strengthening mainly northerly wind from early Tuesday morning, in the morning 8-12 m/s, at its strongest 14 m/s, in the forenoon 9-13 m/s, at noon at its strongest 18 m/s.


The weather is mostly fair, possibly occasional rain showers.
In the evening and at night the weather is comparatively cloudy.
From midnight heavy rain in many places.


From midnight heavy rain in some places, otherwise good visibility.
