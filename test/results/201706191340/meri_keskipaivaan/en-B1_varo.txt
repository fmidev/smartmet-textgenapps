Weather report for Bay of Bothnia on Monday 13 o'clock

Expected weather until Tuesday noon:

Weakening westerly wind, in the beginning 5-8 m/s, on Tuesday morning mainly north-westerly wind, in the forenoon 3-6 m/s.


The weather is rather clear and fair.
On Monday before midnight possibly occasional rain showers.
At night the weather is half cloudy.
From Tuesday morning light rain.


Good visibility.
