Weather report for Eastern Gulf of Finland on Monday 13 o'clock

Expected weather until Tuesday noon:

West to north-westerly wind 8-12 m/s.
Gradually weakening wind from the evening, on Tuesday small hours 4-8 m/s.
Strengthening wind in the forenoon 9-13 m/s.


The weather is half cloudy and fair.
From Tuesday morning rain in many places, some thunder also occurs.
Rain can be heavy in the forenoon.
