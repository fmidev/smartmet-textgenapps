Weather report for Sea of Archipelago on Monday 13 o'clock

Expected weather until Tuesday noon:

Mainly north-westerly wind 3-8 m/s, at its strongest 10 m/s.
South to south-westerly wind from the late evening.
Gradually strengthening wind from early Tuesday morning, in the morning mainly westerly wind 9-13 m/s, in the forenoon 10-14 m/s, at noon at its strongest 18 m/s.


The weather is mostly fair, possibly occasional rain showers.
On Monday evening the weather is half cloudy.
At night and in the morning rain in many places, some thunder also occurs.
Rain can be heavy after midnight and in the morning.
